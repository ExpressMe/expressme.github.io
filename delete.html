Delete Your ExpressMe Account

You can request to delete your ExpressMe account and all associated personal information by sending an email to delete@expressme.nl. When your account is deleted:

  • Your profile and all personal information will be permanently removed
  • All stored preferences, personalized tiles, and uploaded images will be deleted
  • You will no longer have access to the ExpressMe app and its features
  • Any active subscriptions will be canceled

To request account deletion, please:

  • Send an email from the address associated with your ExpressMe account to delete@expressme.nl
  • Include "Account Deletion Request" in the subject line
  • Provide your full name and username in the email

We will process your deletion request and remove all your data within 14 days. You'll receive a confirmation email once the deletion is complete.

Important Information

This action cannot be undone. Once your account is deleted, you won't be able to retrieve any of your personalized content or settings. If you wish to use ExpressMe again in the future, you'll need to create a new account.

If you're experiencing technical issues or just want to take a break, consider keeping your account and just uninstalling the app instead. This way, your personalized content will be preserved for when you want to return.

Do you have questions about deleting your account? Contact our support team at info@expressme.nl
